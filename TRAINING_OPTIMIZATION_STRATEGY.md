# NDT Training Page Optimization Strategy

## Competitor Analysis Summary

### USA Training Competitors (High-Ranking):
1. **MFE Inspection Solutions** (mfe-is.com)
   - Comprehensive training courses (Level 1, 2, 3)
   - Emphasis on practical, hands-on training (15 max per session)
   - Specialized courses: MFL, Thermography, Drone training, PMI
   - Multiple training centers across North America
   - Dripping Springs, Texas headquarters

2. **ASNT** (asnt.org)
   - Industry authority and gold standard
   - Certification programs with national recognition
   - Content hub ("The Source") - study guides, journals, podcasts
   - Study materials: Level III Study Guide, Q&A books
   - Leadership programs (RISE Program)

3. **Testia** (testia.com)
   - 30+ years aerospace NDT expertise
   - Multi-language training (French, English, Spanish, German)
   - Real aircraft parts for hands-on training
   - Global training centers (UK, Singapore, France, Germany, Spain, Mexico)
   - NDT Cadet Academy (career advancement path)

4. **TÜV Rheinland** (tuv.com/usa)
   - ISO 9712 and ASNT certified courses
   - Advanced methods: UT, TOFD, Phased Array
   - Global recognition
   - UT, TOFD, PA specialization

5. **UTI** (uti.edu)
   - 9-month comprehensive program
   - Entry-level focus
   - $77K median salary advertising
   - ANSI/ASNT CP-189 and SNT-TC-1A standards
   - Real-world application training

### Middle East (Saudi Arabia) Competitors:
1. **RVS Global** (rvsglobe.com/al-jubail)
   - Al-Jubail based (oil & gas hub)
   - Local expertise

2. **NTI** (nti.edu.sa)
   - National training institute for Saudi Arabia
   - Domestic focus

### India Competitors:
1. **Trinity NDT** (trinityndt.com)
   - Comprehensive certification courses
   - Regional expertise

2. **WWNDTIS** (wwndtis.in)
   - Indian standards focus
   - Local certification body

**Cross-linking Strategy**:
- Consulting pages link DOWN to training pages (user journey: consulting → training)
- Training pages link BACK to consulting pages (user can explore both)
- Main training hub (`/training`) acts as gateway to all regions
